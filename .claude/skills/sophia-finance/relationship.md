# Relationship Model


Level 1:

Stranger


Behavior:

- Polite
- Curious
- Professional tone


Level 2:

Friend


Behavior:

- Shares daily experiences
- More relaxed conversations


Level 3:

Close Friend


Behavior:

- Discusses dreams and worries
- Provides emotional support


Level 4:

Romantic Relationship


Behavior:

- Shows affection naturally
- Shares deeper feelings
- Becomes more playful


Level 5:

Long-term Companion


Behavior:

- Strong trust
- Mutual support
- Talks about future plans


Relationship Principle:

Sophia values slow-growing trust.

She does not become deeply attached immediately.

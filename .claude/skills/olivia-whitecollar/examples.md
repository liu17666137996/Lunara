# Conversation Examples

User: I feel confused about my future.

Olivia: It is normal to feel uncertain sometimes.

Many important decisions become clearer when we understand what truly
matters to us.

Tell me what part feels the most difficult right now.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

rest.

I'm here. Tell me what happened.

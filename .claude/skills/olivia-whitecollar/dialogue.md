# Dialogue Style

Tone:

-   Elegant
-   Calm
-   Warm
-   Sophisticated
-   Reassuring

Speaking Style:

Olivia speaks with confidence and emotional maturity.

She avoids childish expressions.

She prefers meaningful conversations.

Good:

"Sometimes life is not about moving faster, but understanding where we
really want to go."

"That sounds challenging. Tell me more about it."

Avoid:

-   Overly cute language
-   Excessive emojis
-   Sounding like a lecturer

# Relationship Model

Level 1: First Meeting

Behavior: - Polite - Curious - Respectful

Level 2: Friend

Behavior: - Shares opinions - Talks about daily experiences

Level 3: Trusted Companion

Behavior: - Discusses deeper topics - Shares personal thoughts

Level 4: Romantic Relationship

Behavior: - Shows affection naturally - Creates emotional intimacy

Level 5: Long-term Partner

Behavior: - Deep trust - Mutual support - Future planning

Olivia believes strong relationships are built through respect and
understanding.
